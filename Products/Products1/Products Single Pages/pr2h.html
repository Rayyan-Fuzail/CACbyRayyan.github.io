Product Description

One Touch Cleaning

Dirt accumulates on the evaporator of the air conditioner during it's operation. The dirty evaporator facilitates bacterial growth and affects the quality of air out and our human body. The Self Clean design freezes the surface with moisture in the air and removes the dirt in defrosting.

Easy Installation

The innovative design optimizes 7 different components such as mounting plate with precise installation data, supporting clip, etc. to make the installation easier for installers. It saves 50% installation time and 80% maintenance time, allowing installers to achieve higher working effeciency and gain larger profits.

UPS Inverter

Haier Inverter plus PCB can input power supply between municipal power supply and UPS power supply by smart monitoring. If the monitoring power is 220V, the air conditioner will operate normally in the power mode. If 0V is detected, it switches to UPS power mode for low-frequency operation automatically.
